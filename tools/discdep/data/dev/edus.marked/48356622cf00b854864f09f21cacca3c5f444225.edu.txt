The novel coronavirus disease-2019 ,
caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus-2 ,
has spread worldwide from China . <S>
There are no case reports from Asia of COVID-19 with facial paralysis and olfactory disturbance . <S>
We herein report a case of COVID-19 pneumonia in a Japanese woman
who showed facial nerve palsy and olfactory disturbance . <S> <P>

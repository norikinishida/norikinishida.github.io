By 11 March 2020 , it was designated a pandemic
owing to its rapid worldwide spread . <S>
In this short article we provide some information
that might be useful
and help equip colleagues
to reduce medical error during a pandemic . <S> <P>
We advocate a systems - based approach , rather than an individual 's sole responsibility ,
and , look at ways
to provide safer healthcare . <S> <P>

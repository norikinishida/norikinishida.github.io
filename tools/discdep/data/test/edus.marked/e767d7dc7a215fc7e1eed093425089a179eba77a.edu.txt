A combination of federal and state - level decision making has shaped the response to COVID-19 in the United States . <S>
In this paper we analyze the Twitter narratives around this decision making
by applying a dynamic topic model to COVID-19 related tweets by U.S. Governors and Presidential cabinet members . <S>
We use a network Hawkes binomial topic model
to track evolving sub - topics around risk , testing and treatment . <S>
We also construct influence networks amongst government officials
using Granger causality
inferred from the network Hawkes process . <S> <P>

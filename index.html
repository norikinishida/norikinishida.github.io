Noriki Nishida is a postdoctoral researcher at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), Japan. He is also a visiting researcher at Nakayama Laboratory at The University of Tokyo. Previously, he receiced his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from The University of Tokyo.
He works in the area of natural language processing and computational linguistics. In particular, he is interested in discourse parsing and automatic analysis of natural language documents. His primary research goal is to understand the structural regularities of coherent natural language text beyond the scope of a sentence and support human communications and knowledge discovery, using discourse parsing technologies.

Noriki Nishida is a researcher at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), Japan. Previously, he received his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from The University of Tokyo. He is working on research and development in natural language processing, with a particular focus on knowledge acquisition, information extraction, and their applications in specialized domains such as medicine and healthcare. He is interested in automatically extracting and organizing useful knowledge from large document collections to facilitate knowledge discovery.

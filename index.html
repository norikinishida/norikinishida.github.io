Research Interests

I work in the area of computational linguistics and natural language processing. I am interested in uncovering linguistic/informational structures and rules from linguistic phenomena in a data-driven manner. Particularly, I am focusing on problems related to automated text parsing. I am also interested in knowledge acquisition from text.

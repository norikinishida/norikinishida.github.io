Noriki Nishida is a postdoctoral researcher at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), Japan. He is also a visiting researcher at Nakayama Laboratory at The University of Tokyo. Previously, he received his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from The University of Tokyo. He works in the are of natural language processing and computational linguistics. He is interested in automatic analysis of natural language text and knowledge acquisition. His complete CV (PDF) can be found here.

Research Areas

  • natural language processing, computational linguistics
  • text analysis (especially discourse parsing)
  • knowledge acquisition

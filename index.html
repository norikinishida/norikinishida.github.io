Noriki Nishida is a postdoctoral researcher at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), Japan. Previously, he receiced his Ph.D. degree in 2020 from The University of Tokyo.
His research is aimed at using computers to analyze natural language text, extract information from it, and support knowledge retrieval and discovery. In particular, he is working on discourse analysis and knowledge acquisition from biomedical papers.
